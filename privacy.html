Privacy Policy

At YoSoko, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number when creating an account.
  • Listing Data: Item titles, descriptions, images, and prices you post.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, and interactions with listings.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To allow users to post and browse items or wanted ads.
  • To enable contact (e.g., WhatsApp, calls) between buyers and sellers.
  • To provide customer support and improve platform functionality.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. We only share contact info (like phone numbers) when it's needed for completing a transaction or enabling communication between users.

4. Firebase & Third-Party Tools

We use Firebase for authentication, database, and image storage. These services are subject to Google's privacy practices.

5. Data Security

We apply secure coding practices and store your data in secure cloud environments. However, no system is 100% immune to risks — please avoid sharing sensitive info unnecessarily.

6. Your Rights

  • You can update your contact info via your profile.
  • You can delete your account by contacting us at support@yosoko.co.tz.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When we do, we’ll post the new version here with an updated date.

8. Contact

For any questions or concerns, reach us at support@yosoko.co.tz.
